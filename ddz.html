iBuilding

iBuilding is a commercial real estate rental platform with large sales teams across various cities. My role was to improve the efficiency of these teams. As both a product and visual designer, I collaborated with managers to create new regulations for bidding processes and career advancement, highlighting these features in both the app and the web back-end.

While working at a company focused primarily on offline sales, I also had opportunities to conduct research on traditional sales models.

Cooperated orders

We encouraged our sales teams to share resources, but we also wanted to prevent order owners from being overwhelmed with requests. To address this, we allowed the owner to select the partners they wanted to collaborate with, ensuring a controlled and effective approach.

Bidding

Incoming customer orders are displayed in iBuilding Assistant, notifying salespeople in real-time. To expedite our response to customers, we introduced bid competitions for order allocation.

Resources & Data

Information about each property is crucial to the sales team. They can access the latest updates and even gain benefits by providing verified information, functioning similarly to Wikipedia for real estate data.

Performance & Promotion

Career growth is often intense, with its fair share of ups and downs. To maintain a positive atmosphere during competitive periods, we designed the career progression system to feel more like a game, making the journey more engaging and less stressful.
