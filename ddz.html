iBuilding

iBuilding is a commercial real estate renting platform, and there are serval huge sales teams in different cities. My goal was to increase the efficiency of our sales teams. As both product and visual designer, I work with the other managers to set the new regulation of bidding order and career promotion, and emphasized these new features in the app and the web back-end.

In a company that focus more on offline sales, I also got chances to participated some researches about traditional sale field.

Cooperated orders

Sharing resources was one of things that we encouraged our sales teams to do, but we don't want to let the order owners encounter too much request which could have been kind of annoying. So we let the owner be the one to select who to work with.

Bidding

Orders coming from customers will show in iBuilding Assitant and notifies salesmans at once, to make the repsonse to our customers even faster, we dicided to turn order distribution into a bid competition.

Resources & Data

Informations about every office are the key to sales. They can get the latest informations here, and also can earn benefits from updating valid information here, like a wikipedia.

Performance & Promotion

Career is always a serious thing, especially when there has to be ups and downs, but we need to keep the competition atmosphese, so we tried to make it more like a game to ease the tension feeling.
