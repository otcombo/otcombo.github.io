WeChat

Designing for one of the world’s largest messaging and social media platforms is like dancing in shackles. Countless ideas and iterations were made to refine every detail, with even stricter standards applied during the redesign.

Our growth isn't measured just by active users or the number of messages sent each day, but by how deeply WeChat integrates into every aspect of a user's lifestyle. That's why we explored various fields with our insights and experience -- like WeChat Reader, WeChat TV, and beyond.

WeChat 6.0 Redesign

For a long time, WeChat was often seen as lacking character, making it difficult for users to describe how they felt about it—even though many used it daily. Defining "what we are" became essential, it's challenging, but ultimately meaningful.

Consistency

Maintaining a clean and efficient feel across all platforms was a priority. We focused on embodying WeChat’s character: concise and elegant.

Branding Color

We adjusted the branding colors, adding more light gray alongside WeChat green to enhance the app’s clean and elegant look.

Icon Style

The icons were updated to a more refined style, replacing the former “cute” appearance to better match WeChat's mature character.

Guidelines

We created design guidelines and templates to standardize outputs, ensuring consistency and optimizing workflow across different teams.

WeChat Redpocket

The debut of Red Pocket during the Spring Festival Gala reached a national audience, creating massive anticipation. We emphasized feedback animations to keep the user experience smooth and to alleviate the anxiety of waiting.

WeChat Login

Logging into WeChat on Mac/PC/iPad required extensive effort to streamline the synchronization of messages and prevent repeated notifications across devices.

WeChat Reader

We aimed to create a better experience for users consuming and sharing media within WeChat. Key aspects focused on content generation and enhancing the reading experience.

WeChat Reader iPhone ver.

Unlike the iPad version, which focuses on providing an optimal reading experience, the iPhone version emphasizes quality recommendations and content sharing.

WeChat Hardware Project

We partnered with manufacturers to explore opportunities for integrating WeChat features into everyday life scenarios.

Style Sheet For Designer

With fluctuating resolution ratios, adaptive design was challenging—especially before we fully embraced auto-layout solutions. We created readable style sheets that allowed designers to adjust specific variables independently.

We also integrated Jenkins to streamline testing and iteration, keeping everything in designers’ hands.
