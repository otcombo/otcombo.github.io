echo

Echo is primarily used for capturing daily life as photos or short videos and sharing with close friends & families, which can be edited with various stickers, text captions etc. Besides from interacting with close friends, echo also allow users to have a peek of the “outside world”, discover strangers who share their interesting moments.

Students in colleges and graduated, who would be encountering an expansion of their social network circles, are our primary target users. New generations have experienced social networking apps of various kinds and would be more likely attracted by new thing, hence, on design aspect, we tried to present something uncommon and 'weird'.

Simplified hierarchy for fullscreen

Traditional horizontal hierarchy desgin would sometime let users get lost in multi-page hierarchy with countless left swipe returns. As the trend of fullscreen goes, finger gestures becomes more convenient since top corners are harder to touch. Making good use of the vertical space can be another hierarchy solution, so we designed the relations between all kinds of module as a slide-up card, close with swiping down.

DUOTONE COVER

Since the list of moments is basically the most primary element that user would see and interact with, we needed to present maximum amount of content, and one of the difficulties we encountered here was the messy feeling that these photo covers would made.

We made a duotone filter to unify the color of photos, adjusting colors according to the status of the moment. The rainbow-like list is also one of the symbolic pattern of echo.

When browsing moments, we want to keep the story-like immersive experience, and fullscreen everything with a strict rules adapting to various resolution ratios. And because of this, we try to make comments can be marked on the photo that related to and let leaving a comment more intuitive. Just tap and hold at wherever you want to leave the comment, and type and done.

Every moment shared after 24hr will be archived in the profile as Memoir. Memories usually are kinda messy, but selected moments shown as Highlight look like a hand of cards well organized.

The goal of our camera is to capture every moment and thoughts in different ways asap. The consistency between different ways here keep the same experience through different media and ensure 'speed' of capture moments and thoughts that we want.

Besides from interacting with close friends, pull down from the top will enter another space full of thoughts and moments shared by strangers about various topics happening right at that moment.

Same horizontal list, but we use words instead of photos because that's much more close to thoughts which would be the majority of contents here. A series of rule were set to limit people here would probably someone have a few connections / similarities, yet you don't know each other.
