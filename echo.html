echo

Echo is primarily used for capturing daily life as photos or short videos and sharing with close friends & families, which can be edited with various stickers, text captions etc. Besides from interacting with close friends, echo also allow users to have a peek of the “outside world”, discover strangers who share their interesting moments.

As the Design Lead for this start-up project, I led not only UI design but also product design on the early stage, and worked on building design team.
